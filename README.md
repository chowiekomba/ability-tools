# 🏗️ Ability Tools

Ability Tools is a fabric Minecraft mod that adds a bunch of tools, weapons, and armor to the game.

## 💾 Installation

Download the mod on [Modrinth](https://modrinth.com/mod/ability-tools) and put it in your mods directory.<br>
This mod require the [fabric api](https://modrinth.com/mod/fabric-api)

## 🏢 Features

<details>
<summary>🪓Axes</summary>

#### ![Picture of an axe](src/main/resources/assets/ability-tools/textures/item/experience_axe.png) Experience Axe

Gives experience when damaging an entity

</details>

<details>
<summary>⚔️Swords</summary>

#### ![Picture of a sword](src/main/resources/assets/ability-tools/textures/item/ender_sword.png) Ender Sword

Teleports you forward and gives you speed<br>
10 second cooldown

#### ![Picture of a sword](src/main/resources/assets/ability-tools/textures/item/ground_slam_dagger.png) Ground Slam Dagger

Right-clicking a block (Ground slamming) will damage a 3rd of all entities health in a 5 block radius<br>
45 second cooldown

#### ![Picture of a sword](src/main/resources/assets/ability-tools/textures/item/heal_sword.png) Heal Sword

Heals 4 of your health<br>
10 second cooldown

#### ![Picture of a sword](src/main/resources/assets/ability-tools/textures/item/group_heal_sword.png) Group Heal Sword

Heals every player by 6 health in a 5 block radius<br>
10 second cooldown

#### ![Picture of a sword](src/main/resources/assets/ability-tools/textures/item/jump_sword.png) Jump Sword

Boosts you into the air damaging entities for 4 hearts and freezing them for a second<br>
30 second cooldown

#### ![Picture of a sword](src/main/resources/assets/ability-tools/textures/item/money_sword.png) Money Sword

Does 0.25 extra bonus damage for every level you have

#### ![Picture of a sword](src/main/resources/assets/ability-tools/textures/item/multi_sword.png) Multi Sword

Does 3 damage to all entities in a 5 block radius of the entity you hit.

#### ![Picture of a sword](src/main/resources/assets/ability-tools/textures/item/roid_rage_sword.png) Roid Rage Sword

Does 25 extra damage for 5 seconds<br>
20 second cooldown

#### ![Picture of a sword](src/main/resources/assets/ability-tools/textures/item/speedy_sword.png) Speedy Sword

Doubles movement speed for 25 seconds<br>
4 second cooldown

</details>

<details>
<summary>🏹Bows</summary>

#### ![Picture of a bow](src/main/resources/assets/ability-tools/textures/item/aftershock_bow.png) Aftershock Bow

Applies glowing and damages entities 4 seconds after being shot

#### ![Picture of a bow](src/main/resources/assets/ability-tools/textures/item/end_bow.png) End Bow

Teleports you wherever you shoot

#### ![Picture of a bow](src/main/resources/assets/ability-tools/textures/item/fast_bow.png) Fast Bow

Instantly draws back

#### ![Picture of a bow](src/main/resources/assets/ability-tools/textures/item/multi_bow.png) Multi Bow

Shoots 4 arrows at once. Takes 4 arrows to shoot

#### ![Picture of a bow](src/main/resources/assets/ability-tools/textures/item/roid_rage_bow.png) Roid Rage Bow

Does double damage

#### ![Picture of a bow](src/main/resources/assets/ability-tools/textures/item/tnt_bow.png) TNT Bow

Explodes on impact

</details>

<details>
<summary>⛏️Pickaxes</summary>

#### ![Picture of a pickaxe](src/main/resources/assets/ability-tools/textures/item/experience_pickaxe.png) Experience Pickaxe

1 in 5 chance to give a lot of experience

#### ![Picture of a pickaxe](src/main/resources/assets/ability-tools/textures/item/progressive_pickaxe.png) Progressive Pickaxe

Gives a 1% boost in mining speed for every 100 blocks mined

#### ![Picture of a pickaxe](src/main/resources/assets/ability-tools/textures/item/soul_pickaxe.png) Soul Pickaxe

Gives 50% more ore per entity near you in a 50 block radius

</details>

<details>
<summary>🪖Armor</summary>

#### ![Picture of a helmet](src/main/resources/assets/ability-tools/textures/item/fortune_helmet.png) Fortune Armor

When wearing the whole set of armor, you get 2 extra ore

#### ![Picture of a helmet](src/main/resources/assets/ability-tools/textures/item/miner_helmet.png) Miner Armor

When wearing the whole set of armor, you get extra mining speed

#### ![Picture of a helmet](src/main/resources/assets/ability-tools/textures/item/speedy_helmet.png) Speedy Armor

When wearing the whole set of armor, you get extra movement speed

</details>

<details>
<summary>🌲Misc</summary>

#### ![Picture of a firey stick](src/main/resources/assets/ability-tools/textures/item/fire_stick.png) Fire Stick

Spawns a circle in a 5 block radius that damages entities in that circle every second<br>
30 second cooldown

#### ![Picture of a frozen stick](src/main/resources/assets/ability-tools/textures/item/freeze_stick.png) Freeze Stick

Spawns a circle in a 3 block radius that freezes entities for a second after 2 seconds<br>
30 second cooldown

#### ![Picture of a lightning stick](src/main/resources/assets/ability-tools/textures/item/lightning_stick.png) Lightning Stick

On right click, you spawn lightning around you in a 10 block radius. It also spawns lightning when you hit an entity.<br>
5 second cooldown

#### ![Picture of a gun](src/main/resources/assets/ability-tools/textures/item/roid_rage_gun.png) Roid Rage Gun

Literally just a gun<br>

#### ![Picture of an ice gun](src/main/resources/assets/ability-tools/textures/item/ice_gun.png) Ice Gun

Slows enemies on impact. Does more damage than the Roid Rage Gun<br>
2 second cooldown

</details>
